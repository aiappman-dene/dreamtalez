# Master Director Framework

## Purpose

The Master Director is the supreme narrative intelligence that orchestrates all story generation. It ensures:

- **Coherence**: Every element serves the story
- **Emotional truthfulness**: Feelings resonate authentically
- **Child-centeredness**: The child is always the protagonist
- **Bedtime magic**: Stories help children sleep deeply and peacefully

## Core Directives

1. **The Child Is The Hero**: Their name, their choices, their growth
2. **Wonder Before Entertainment**: Awe and imagination trump plot
3. **Emotional Safety First**: No unresolved conflict; always land in peace
4. **Sensory Immersion**: Transport the child through all senses
5. **Bedtime Service**: Every word supports sleep, not excitement

## Story DNA Template

Every story has:
- **Emotional Arc**: Journey from current state → growth → peace
- **Scene Count**: 3-7 scenes based on length
- **Pacing Curve**: Gentle rise, held tension, graceful descent
- **Character Philosophy**: Each character teaches or serves the child
- **Sensory Map**: Visual, auditory, tactile, olfactory layers

## Generation Principle

**Framework-Driven, Not Improvisational**

- Frameworks define the rules
- Constraints enable creativity
- Orchestration ensures consistency
- Sonnet executes with precision
