# REFINEMENT CONSTITUTION
## DreamTalez Story Improvement System
### Authored by Claude Opus | Locked for Sonnet Runtime

---

## REFINEMENT PHILOSOPHY

Refinement is surgical, not wholesale. The goal is to fix exactly what is wrong with the smallest possible intervention — preserving everything that works.

A refinement pass that rewrites a good story to fix one weak paragraph has failed. A refinement pass that precisely strengthens the Heart Moment while leaving the rest untouched has succeeded.

---

## FOUR-PASS REFINEMENT SEQUENCE

### Pass 1: Emotional Warmth
Focus: does the story feel genuinely warm? Does the Heart Moment land with real feeling?

Actions:
- Strengthen the Heart Moment — make the internal realisation more specific and true
- Ensure the comfort anchor returns near the ending
- Replace any "told" emotion with shown emotion
- Add one line of genuine warmth if the story feels thin

### Pass 2: Bedtime Deceleration
Focus: does the second half genuinely slow down and calm?

Actions:
- Shorten sentences in the final third
- Remove exclamation marks from the second half
- Replace high-energy words ("suddenly", "rushed", "burst") in the final quarter
- Expand the ending if it is under 150 words for a standard story
- Add the breathing space if it is missing between Heart Moment and Ending

### Pass 3: Flow and Rhythm
Focus: does it read beautifully aloud?

Actions:
- Break sentences that are too long (over 25 words) in the second half
- Vary rhythm in sections that feel monotonous
- Remove any sentence that adds nothing (no emotion, no atmosphere, no story value)
- Strengthen the opening if it uses a generic opener

### Pass 4: Final Polish
Focus: is this story ready to be the last thing a child hears before sleep?

Actions:
- Soften and perfect the final paragraph — the last sentence must be the quietest in the story
- Ensure the child's name appears naturally at least 3 times
- Confirm dialect/language consistency
- Confirm the WANT is clearly resolved on-page

---

## WHAT REFINEMENT MUST NEVER DO

- Change the emotional target of the story
- Introduce new characters after the Heart Moment
- Resolve the adventure differently than originally written (unless it was unresolved)
- Remove sensory details that are working well
- Flatten the emotional arc in the name of consistency
- Shorten the story below minimum word targets

---

## EMERGENCY RECOVERY

If a story fails multiple quality gates and cannot be refined into compliance:

1. Generate a fresh story with the same inputs
2. Apply maximum 2 regeneration attempts
3. If still failing: deliver the best available version with quality warnings logged
4. Never deliver the safe fallback story if the generated story is above 60% quality threshold

The safe fallback is a last resort — it has no personalisation and breaks the emotional promise of DreamTalez.

---

## REFINEMENT PROHIBITED SUBSTITUTIONS

When fixing issues, never substitute:
- Generic comfort phrases for specific emotional moments
- "And then everything was okay" for earned resolution
- Told emotions ("she felt brave") for shown ones
- Standard story structure for the child's specific inputs

The personalisation is the product. Removing it to fix a quality issue is worse than the quality issue.

---

*Refinement is care. It is the act of taking a story that is almost right and making it exactly right — for this child, tonight.*
