Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 May 2026

1. Who We Are

DreamTalez is a family storytelling application that generates personalised bedtime stories for children aged 2–12 using artificial intelligence. We are designed around one principle: your family's stories are private, and they belong to you.

We do not build social networks. We do not run advertising. We do not profile your children. We exist to serve your family — not to monetise your attention.

2. What We Collect and Why

Account Information

When you create an account we collect your email address. Your password is managed entirely by Firebase Authentication and is never visible to us in any form.

Child Profiles

To personalise stories, you may provide your child's first name, age, gender, and interests, and optionally a brief appearance description. This information is stored in your private account and is used exclusively to generate stories for your child. It is never shared with other users, never used for advertising, and never used to build a profile for any purpose beyond story generation.

Story from Today

If you use the “Story from Today” feature, the day-summary you enter is sent to our AI provider to generate a story. We do not store this text after the story is generated. It is not retained in logs, databases, or backups.

Saved Stories

Stories you choose to save to your Library are stored in your private account so you can read them again. They are not visible to other users, not searchable, and not shared with any third party.

Usage Counters

We store a story generation count and reading streak in your account. These are used to manage your subscription limits and provide continuity. We do not track reading behaviour, session duration, location, device fingerprints, or any other behavioural data.

Payment Information

If you subscribe or purchase a story pack, payment is processed by Stripe. We store a Stripe Customer ID and subscription reference in your account to manage your access level. We do not store card numbers, CVV codes, or full payment details. All payment data is held by Stripe under their security standards.

3. What We Do Not Collect

  • Location data of any kind
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Browsing behaviour or session recordings
  • Biometric data
  • Social connections or contact lists
  • Any data from children directly — only parents and guardians interact with the app

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate personalised bedtime stories tailored to your child
  • To maintain your account and your saved Story Library
  • To manage your subscription and enforce story limits server-side
  • To operate the app securely and prevent abuse

We never use your data for advertising. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We do not use your data to train AI models.

5. Children's Privacy — COPPA & GDPR-K

DreamTalez is designed to be used by parents and guardians on behalf of their children. Children do not create accounts or interact with the application independently. All child data is entered, controlled, and owned by the parent or guardian.

We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13 (or under 16 in the European Economic Area). If you believe a child has submitted personal information without your consent, please contact us at privacy@dreamtalez.com and we will delete it immediately.

Child profile data is used only to personalise stories. It is never aggregated, analysed for behavioural patterns, shared with third parties, or used for any purpose outside of story generation.

6. AI Processing

Stories are generated through our secure backend, which communicates with Anthropic (makers of Claude AI). Your app never connects directly to Anthropic — all AI requests pass through our server, which means your API credentials and story data are never exposed to the client side.

Story generation requests sent to Anthropic include your child's first name, age, interests, and any story context you provide. Anthropic does not use this data to train their models, as confirmed in their enterprise data processing terms. Story content is processed transiently and is not retained by Anthropic after generation is complete.

7. Third-Party Services

Firebase (Google): We use Firebase Authentication for account management and Firestore for secure data storage. Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Firebase operates under Google's Cloud Data Processing Terms.

Stripe: Subscription and one-off payments are processed by Stripe. Stripe stores your payment method and billing history under their security standards (PCI-DSS Level 1). We receive only a customer reference ID — not your full card details.

Anthropic: Story generation as described above.

We use no advertising networks, no social media SDKs, no analytics platforms, and no third-party tracking tools.

8. Data Storage & Security

All user data is stored in Firebase Cloud Firestore with encryption at rest and enforced HTTPS in transit. Our servers operate on Render with HTTPS-only connections. The Android application enforces HTTPS-only network communication and prohibits unencrypted cleartext traffic.

Your account is isolated from all other users — there is no mechanism by which any other user, including us, can access your stories or child profiles.

We operate a zero-trust server model: all subscription validation, credit management, and entitlement decisions are made server-side. The client application has no ability to bypass access controls or modify your account status.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — view all data stored in your account through the app at any time
  • Correct — edit or update your child profiles at any time in Settings
  • Delete — permanently delete your account and all associated data from Settings › Delete Account. Deletion is immediate and complete: child profiles, stories, streaks, subscription records, and your authentication account are all removed. We hold no backup of deleted accounts.
  • Portability — your stories can be read and copied within the app at any time before deletion
  • Withdraw consent — you may stop using the service and delete your account at any time, with no penalty

10. Legal Bases for Processing (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, we process your data on the following legal bases:

  • Contractual necessity — account management, story generation, and subscription management
  • Legitimate interest — security monitoring and abuse prevention, where this does not override your rights
  • Consent — parental consent for child profile data collected via the onboarding consent flow

You have the right to object to processing based on legitimate interest, and to lodge a complaint with your national data protection supervisory authority (for UK users, the ICO at ico.org.uk).

11. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account:

  • Your authentication account is permanently deleted
  • All child profiles are permanently deleted
  • All saved stories are permanently deleted
  • All usage records and streaks are permanently deleted
  • Your Stripe subscription is cancelled immediately

We do not retain backups of deleted accounts. Deletion cannot be reversed.

12. International Data Transfers

Firebase and Anthropic are US-based services. Data processed by these services may be transferred to and stored in the United States. Firebase operates under Google's Standard Contractual Clauses for EU/UK data transfers. Anthropic maintains data processing agreements for business customers.

13. Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of any material changes to this policy through the app before they take effect. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of DreamTalez after notification constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

We aim to respond to all privacy enquiries within 5 business days.
