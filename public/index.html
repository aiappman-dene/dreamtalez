Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 May 2026

1. Who We Are

DreamTalez is a family storytelling app that generates personalised bedtime stories for children aged 2–12. We are built around one principle: your family's stories are private, and they belong to you. We do not run advertising. We do not profile your children. We exist to serve your family.

2. What We Collect

Account: Your email address. Your password is managed by Firebase Authentication and is never visible to us.

Child profiles: First name, age, gender, interests, and optionally a brief appearance description — used only to personalise stories, stored only in your private account.

Story from Today: The day-summary you enter is sent to our AI to generate a story. We do not store this text after the story is generated.

Saved stories: Stories you save to your Library are stored in your account and are never visible to other users.

Usage counters: Story generation count and reading streak, used to manage your subscription. No behavioural tracking, no location, no device identifiers.

Payment: Stripe processes payments. We store only a Stripe Customer ID — never your card number or CVV.

3. What We Do Not Collect

  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Browsing behaviour or session recordings
  • Any data directly from children — only parents and guardians use the app

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate personalised bedtime stories for your child
  • To maintain your account and Story Library
  • To manage your subscription server-side
  • To operate securely and prevent abuse

We never use your data for advertising. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We do not use your data to train AI models.

5. Children's Privacy — COPPA & GDPR-K

DreamTalez is used by parents and guardians on behalf of their children. Children do not create accounts or interact with the app independently. All child data is controlled by the parent or guardian.

We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13 (or under 16 in the EEA). If you believe a child has submitted data without your consent, email privacy@dreamtalez.com and we will delete it immediately.

6. AI Processing

Stories are generated through our secure backend, which communicates with Anthropic (Claude AI). Your app never connects to Anthropic directly — all AI requests pass through our server. Anthropic does not use this data to train their models and processes it transiently.

7. Third-Party Services

Firebase (Google): Authentication and Firestore storage. Data encrypted at rest and in transit.

Stripe: Secure payment processing. PCI-DSS Level 1 certified.

Anthropic: Story generation as described above.

We use no advertising networks, no social media SDKs, and no tracking tools.

8. Security

All data is encrypted at rest and in transit via HTTPS. Your account is completely isolated from other users. All subscription validation and credit decisions are made server-side — the app cannot bypass access controls.

9. Your Rights

  • Access — view your data in the app at any time
  • Correct — edit child profiles in Settings
  • Delete — Settings › Delete Account removes everything immediately and permanently: profiles, stories, streaks, subscription, and your authentication account. No backup is retained.
  • Portability — your stories can be read and copied within the app before deletion

10. Legal Bases (GDPR)

For EEA and UK users: we process data on the basis of contractual necessity (service delivery), legitimate interest (security and abuse prevention), and consent (child profile data via parental consent flow). You may lodge a complaint with your national supervisory authority — UK users: ico.org.uk.

11. Data Retention

Data is retained while your account is active. Account deletion is immediate and complete. We hold no backups of deleted accounts.

12. Changes

We will notify you in-app before making material changes. Continued use after notification constitutes acceptance.

13. Contact

Email: privacy@dreamtalez.com
