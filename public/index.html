    Privacy Policy

    Last updated: 12 April 2026

    1. Who We Are

    DreamTalez ("we", "us", "our") is a mobile application that generates personalised bedtime stories for children aged 2–12 using artificial intelligence. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users and, in particular, the children for whom stories are created.

    2. Information We Collect

    Account information: When you sign up we collect your email address and a password (stored securely by Firebase Authentication).

    Child profiles: You may provide your child's first name, age, gender, interests, and a physical appearance description. This information is used solely to personalise stories and is stored in your private account.

    "Story from Today" entries: If you use the Story from Today feature, the day-summary text you enter is sent to our AI provider to generate a story. We do not store these entries after the story is generated.

    Generated stories: Stories you save to your Library are stored in your account so you can read them again.

    Usage data: We collect basic analytics such as story generation counts and reading streaks. We do not track browsing behaviour, location, or device identifiers.

    3. How We Use Your Information

    • To create personalised bedtime stories tailored to your child
    • To maintain your account and saved Story Library
    • To manage your subscription and free trial
    • To improve the quality of our story generation

    We never use your data for advertising. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

    4. Children's Privacy (COPPA & GDPR-K)

    DreamTalez is designed for use by parents and guardians on behalf of their children. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13.

    All child profile information is entered and controlled by the parent or guardian. Children do not create accounts or interact with the app independently.

    If you believe a child has provided us with personal information without parental consent, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

    5. Third-Party Services

    Firebase (Google): We use Firebase for authentication and data storage. Firebase processes your email address and stored data under Google's privacy policies.

    Anthropic (Claude AI): Story generation requests are sent to Anthropic's Claude API. These requests include the child's first name, age, interests, appearance description, and any story prompts you provide. Anthropic processes this data under their privacy policy and does not use it to train their models.

    We do not use any advertising, tracking, or social media SDKs.

    6. Data Storage & Security

    Your data is stored securely using Firebase Cloud Firestore with encryption at rest and in transit. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We use HTTPS for all communications.

    7. Your Rights

    You have the right to:

    • Access your personal data at any time through the app
    • Correct your child profiles by editing them in the app
    • Delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings
    • Export your stories by reading them within the app

    When you delete your account, all data including child profiles, stories, streaks, and account information is permanently removed from our systems.

    8. Data Retention

    We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all data is permanently deleted immediately. We do not retain backups of deleted accounts.

    9. International Users

    If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, we process your data under the legal basis of contractual necessity (to provide the service you signed up for) and legitimate interest (to improve our service). You have additional rights under GDPR including the right to data portability and the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

    10. Changes to This Policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app. Your continued use of DreamTalez after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

    11. Contact Us

    If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at:

    Email: privacy@dreamtalez.com
