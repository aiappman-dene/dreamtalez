Our Commitment

Bedtalez is designed for children and families. We are fully committed to privacy, safety, and accessibility for all users.

Privacy & Compliance

  • COPPA Compliant: We do not collect more information than necessary and never share children’s data.
  • GDPR Compliant: All data is stored securely. You can request deletion at any time.
  • Parental Consent: We require parental consent for all accounts and story creation.
  • No Ads, No Tracking: We do not show ads or use third-party trackers.

Accessibility

  • Dyslexia Friendly Mode: Toggle a font and layout designed for dyslexic readers.
  • Neurodivergent Friendly Mode: Calmer colors, reduced animation, and simplified UI.
  • Screen Reader Support: All content is accessible via screen readers.

Questions or requests? Email support@bedtalez.com
